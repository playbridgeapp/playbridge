# Privacy Policy for PlayBridge

**Effective Date: April 18, 2026**

PlayBridge ("we," "us," or "our") provides the PlayBridge application for Phone and TV. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use our services.

## 1. Information Collection
PlayBridge is designed as a local-first casting and media playback tool.
- **Local Network Activity**: We use your local network to discover and connect between the PlayBridge Phone app and the PlayBridge TV app.
- **Media URLs**: We process media URLs you choose to play. These URLs are sent from your phone to your TV over your local network.
- **No Personal Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, account information, or contact lists to external servers.

## 2. Information Usage
- **Playback Coordination**: Your media choices and playback state (position, duration) are synchronized between your devices to provide a seamless remote control experience.
- **Stremio Addons**: If you use Stremio addons, the TV app fetches stream information directly from those addons as configured by you.

## 3. Third-Party Services
PlayBridge acts as a receiver for media content. Third-party services (such as video hosts or Stremio addons) may have their own privacy policies regarding the data they collect when you stream content from them.

## 4. Local Storage
- **Settings & Tokens**: We store an authentication token and basic settings (preferred language, filter presets) locally on your device to facilitate pairing and consistent playback experience.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
